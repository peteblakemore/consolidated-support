Pay and transfer apprenticeship funds

Apprenticeship service account

You can get an account for the apprenticeship service if you’re:

  • a levy paying employer
  • an employer receiving a transfer

Training provider costs

When you take on an apprentice, you agree the total cost with the training provider. The amount could change if there are any changes to the apprenticeship.

Your provider can change the apprenticeship start and end dates without your approval, but this will not change the total cost. To make any other changes your provider must send a request to your account.

Transfers

Levy-paying employers can transfer up to 25% of their annual apprenticeship funds to other employers. This can help other businesses or charities pay for their apprenticeships.

You’ll lose your apprenticeship funds if you don’t spend or transfer them within 2 years.

You can view your transfer allowance in your apprenticeship service account. It’s calculated using your total declared levy from the previous tax year, yourEnglish percentage and government top-up.

Choose an employer

You must choose an employer to transfer your funds to. This can be any levy or non-levy paying employer, including apprenticeship training agencies (ATA).

Before you transfer funds from your apprenticeship account, you need to:

  • agree the cost, apprenticeship standard and number of apprentices with the employer
  • sign an ESFA Apprenticeship Employer Agreement
  • connect your accounts

Connect your accounts

You need to ask the receiving employer for their account ID so you can connect your accounts.

Enter the ID into the new transfers section of your account. The name and details of the employer you are connecting with will show.

After you connect accounts, the receiving employer and their training provider can add apprentices to the service. We’ll ask you to approve the cost, standard and number of apprentices.

Transfer your funds

After the training provider submits their apprenticeship data, payments will be made automatically every month. You can view the transactions in the finance section of your account.

You cannot stop transferring the funds before the apprenticeship ends. Funds will transfer unless:

  • the apprenticeship finishes
  • the receiving employer stops the transfer

Transferred funds will come out of your account first every month. Make sure your account has enough funds for all of your financial commitments.
