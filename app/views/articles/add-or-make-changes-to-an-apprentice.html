Add or make changes to an apprentice

After you recruit a new apprentice, you need to add them to your organisation’s apprenticeship account. You’ll need:

  • the apprentice’s name and date of birth
  • the start and end date of the apprenticeship
  • the cost of the apprenticeship
  • your training provider’s UK Provider Reference Number (UKPRN)

If you need to add multiple apprentices you can upload their details together using our template.

Change an apprentice's details

If your apprentice takes a break or stops learning you must update their details. This will either temporarily or permanently stop payments to their training provider. The training provider must also make any changes in their ILR submission.

Use a training provider

Only providers on the Register of Apprenticeship Training Providers can deliver apprenticeship training.

Search for apprenticeships to view the approved providers that can deliver training for your apprentice.

When your apprentice has a training provider, you can allow them to add apprentices to your organisation and set up payments. You must approve any information your training provider gives us.
